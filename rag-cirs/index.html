Project overview

Enterprise-grade compliance assistant for FAA Advisory Circulars. Regulatory PDFs are parsed, structured, and indexed for citation-first retrieval, producing verifiable answers with linked source passages suitable for safety-critical decision making.

Business impact

Cuts the cost of regulatory research by dramatically reducing manual lookup time while improving consistency and audit readiness. Enables compliance teams to scale coverage without proportional increases in headcount or specialist support.

My role: Strategy ‚Üí architecture ‚Üí build ‚Üí test

Owned the end-to-end system, from solution framing and architecture through ingestion, retrieval design, evaluation, and delivery. Focused on production patterns, traceability, and confidence signaling rather than demo-only behavior.

Scalability

Designed to extend beyond FAA Advisory Circulars to manuals, SOPs, MELs, training content, and internal policy libraries. The architecture transfers cleanly to other compliance-heavy industries such as healthcare, finance, and energy.
