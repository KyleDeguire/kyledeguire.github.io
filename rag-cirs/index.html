Project overview

Retrieval-augmented assistant for FAA Advisory Circulars. Documents are parsed, cleaned, chunked, and embedded in Pinecone; a LangChain retriever routes queries to OpenAI and returns cited answers with source snippets.

Business impact

Helps aviation teams find and interpret regulations faster with cited, readable summaries that improve audit readiness and decision confidence.

My role: Strategy → architecture → build → test

Built the ingestion pipeline for AC PDFs, text cleaning and chunking, embeddings to Pinecone, and a LangChain QA chain with citations. Added evaluation spot-checks and a lightweight demo interface.

Scalability

Extend across airline operations, maintenance manuals, MELs, training programs, and regulatory reporting—or adapt to other compliance-heavy domains (finance, healthcare, energy).

Technical details

OpenAILangChainPineconePDF ingestionChunking + EmbeddingsCitations
  • Data & inputs – FAA Advisory Circular PDFs parsed, cleaned, and normalized with document metadata.
  • Model & processing – structure-aware chunking; embeddings created and stored with section-level metadata.
  • Vector store – Pinecone index for semantic retrieval.
  • Retriever & QA – LangChain pipeline queries vectors and composes grounded answers with citations.
  • Evaluation – spot checks with held-out questions to tune chunk sizes and prompts.
  • Interface – lightweight demo surface showing answer plus linked source passages.
