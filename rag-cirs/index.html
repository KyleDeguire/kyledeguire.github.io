RAG CIRS (Planned)

RAG CIRS (Compliance Information Retrieval System) is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation architecture designed to help regulated organizations instantly locate and verify compliance standards across large document sets. It combines document embedding, retrieval, and LLM-based reasoning to generate validated, citation-backed responses.

This version focuses on an FAA-style compliance use case, mapping the ingestion, retrieval, and grounding workflow across advisory circulars and technical manuals.
