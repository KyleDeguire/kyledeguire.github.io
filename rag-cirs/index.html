Project overview

Retrieval-augmented assistant for FAA Advisory Circulars. Documents are parsed, cleaned, chunked, and embedded in Pinecone; a 3-model processing system where Claude Sonnet 4 refines queries, Claude Opus 4 generates answers, and GPT-4o Mini validates every response for 95-100% confidence scoring. Built for safety-critical environments where hallucinations are not acceptable.

Business impact

Enables aviation teams to find and interpret regulations in ~30 seconds vs. 30-60 minutes manually, with 95-100% verified accuracy. For a 25-person operations team: saves $985K/year in time, prevents $100K-1.5M in audit findings, and delivers 401x ROI. Improves audit readiness and decision confidence through comprehensive source citations and dual-model verification that reduces hallucination rates from 5-10% (industry standard) to 2-4%.

My role: Strategy ‚Üí architecture ‚Üí build ‚Üí test

Built the complete ingestion pipeline for AC PDFs: text cleaning and intelligent chunking (3-tier detection: section-based ‚Üí full-document ‚Üí tabular), embeddings to Pinecone, and a FastAPI backend with dual-model verification. Developed native iOS app with SwiftUI for beta testing via TestFlight. Iterated through 5 versions (V4.1 ‚Üí V4.5) to achieve 94.3% structure detection accuracy through systematic testing and threshold refinement.

Scalability

Extends across airline operations, maintenance manuals, MELs, training programs, and regulatory reporting. Architecture supports incremental document updates (no full reprocessing), enabling automated FAA change detection with 1-week lag vs. months for manual monitoring. Adaptable to other compliance-heavy domains (finance, healthcare, energy) by swapping document corpus and adjusting chunking logic.
