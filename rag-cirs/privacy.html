Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Overview

FAA Reg Search is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices in clear, straightforward terms.

Information We Collect

We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Query Data

  • Your search queries are sent to our backend API for processing
  • Queries are processed in real-time and not stored
  • No user accounts, authentication, or login required

Third-Party Services

AI Processing

  • Queries are processed using Anthropic Claude AI and OpenAI APIs
  • These services process queries according to their respective privacy policies
  • No personally identifiable information is included in API requests

Analytics

  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not collect device information or usage statistics

Data Storage

  • No user data is stored on our servers
  • No query history is maintained
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for professional use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
