AI Weekly Audio Feed

Overview

Automated Weekly Agentic workflow GPT + ElevenLabs

Project overview

Automated weekly review of seven leading AI publications. This agentic workflow curates, summarizes, and narrates major updates using GPT-driven analysis structured around five business criteria. Generates a weekly podcast with transcript and citations for reference or deeper research.

Business impact

Keeps busy executives current by converting fast-moving, complex AI developments into concise audio briefings—reducing screen time, improving retention, and enabling hands-free learning during commutes or travel.

My role: Strategy → architecture → build → test

Designed and deployed the end-to-end agentic workflow linking GPT summarization, ElevenLabs TTS, and Make automations. Built logic for data collection, summarization, and publishing, and tested the full pipeline from input sources to RSS distribution.

Scalability

Highly flexible: adjust subject matter, sub-topics, cadence, and episode length for domains like finance, healthcare, education, or policy. Can localize by region, add sponsor reads, or produce multi-language feeds.
