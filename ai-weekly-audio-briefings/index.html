Project overview

Automated weekly review of leading AI publications. The workflow filters noise, summarizes updates by business criteria, and delivers a concise audio briefing with a transcript and citations.

Business impact

Keeps busy executives current by converting ever-changing AI news into short, high-signal audio briefs, reducing screen time and improving retention.

My role

Strategy → architecture → build → test. Designed the flow, built summarization prompts, integrated ElevenLabs, and automated the weekly build and publish.

Scalability

Adjust domain, cadence, and length; localize by region; add sponsor reads; or produce multi-language feeds.

Technical details

OpenAIElevenLabsPython GitHub ActionsGitHub PagesRSS / feed.xml
  • Data & inputs – weekly automated collection of seven leading AI publications.
  • Model & processing – OpenAI condenses and structures updates into business-focused summaries.
  • Runtime – scheduled GitHub Actions generates audio, transcript, and updates the feed.
  • Audio & delivery – ElevenLabs TTS produces MP3 narration with aligned transcripts for QC.
  • Hosting & distribution – MP3s + RSS on GitHub Pages for podcast apps.
  • Versioning & publish – workflow commits audio and metadata for repeatable releases.

Best experienced with headphones; episodes are ~3–5 minutes.
