AI Journey Blog
A companion to the applied AI projects. These decks show how formal training, experiments, and reflection connect strategy work with real-world AI builds.

Overview

This AI Journey series tracks the path from strategy and leadership work into hands-on AI and back again. Each entry marks a milestone where coursework or a build changed how I think about using AI in actual organizations.

Project overview

Three short slide decks that document key phases of the journey: building and deploying VisionAI, completing Wharton’s AI for Business specialization, and finishing IBM’s RAG and LangChain course while designing a compliance-focused RAG system.

Business impact

For hiring managers and partners, these decks make the path visible. They connect theory to practice, show how I reason about risk and value, and give context for the demos on the main portfolio page.

My role

I scoped each phase, chose the courses and projects, built the demos, and wrote the reflections. The focus is on learning how to ship AI work that is understandable to non-technical stakeholders.

Scalability

New entries can be added as more projects and training stack up. The same pattern can be reused for client-facing journey decks that explain how their AI adoption efforts mature over time.
