VisionAI: Emotion Detection

Deep learning model that predicts facial emotion from images. Trained with TensorFlow and Keras, exported to TensorFlow Lite (TFLite), and deployed to iPhone via Xcode. OpenCV was used for image preprocessing and detection in development.

Tools: TensorFlow, Keras, OpenCV, NumPy, Pandas, TensorFlow Lite (TFLite), Xcode
